Some users have received the following system message:
 
`Hey, we noticed some unusual Quest activity on your account. Quest rewards are meant for people who genuinely complete the tasks, such as playing a game or watching a video, and something didn’t add up on our end. Relying on scripts, tools, or other methods to fake Quest completion is a violation of our Inauthentic Engagement policy. We’re not taking any action from this warning, but if it keeps happening, we may need to limit your Quest access. Tap Learn More to review our policies.`
 
There isn't much I can do to make the script undetected, so use it at your own risk, as you most likely WILL get flagged by doing so.

**How to use this script:**
 1. Accept a quest under the Quests tab
 2. Press Ctrl+Shift+I to open DevTools
 3. Go to the Console tab

5. Follow the printed instructions depending on what type of quest you have
 - If your quest says to "play" the game or watch a video, you can just wait and do nothing
 - If your quest says to "stream" the game, join a vc with a friend or alt and stream any window
 6. Wait a bit for it to complete the quest
7. You can now claim the reward!
You can track the progress by looking at the Quest progress: prints in the Console tab, or by looking at the progress bar in the quests tab.
